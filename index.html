About Me

Chris Tolleson is a project manager and civil engineer with more than 15 years of experience in engineering design, project management, and project controls in the aviation, construction, and nuclear industries.

Currently, Chris serves as the Mid-Atlantic Sector Manager and Deputy Program Manager at Charlotte-Douglas International Airport (CLT), where he performs Project Management, Estimating, Design Review, Contract Negotiation, and Construction Administration services under TYLin's on-call Project & Construction Management contract.

Prior to joining TYLin, Chris worked as a Professional Design Engineer in the commercial nuclear power industry and led his own engineering consulting firm, Tolleson Engineering Consultants in Granbury, Texas.

Chris is currently pursuing graduate studies in Construction and Facilities Management at UNC Charlotte, with a focus on AI applications in the built environment.
