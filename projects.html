Togal.AI Estimating Project

Comprehensive evaluation of Togal.AI's AI-powered construction takeoff capabilities across multiple building types including a multi-story hotel, youth center, fire station, building elevation, and military office.

Key Findings

  • Strong on floor plans — Good polygon accuracy for standard room configurations
  • Struggles with foundations & elevations — Requires significant manual correction
  • Misses specialty items — Fixtures, equipment, and unique features need manual addition
  • Valuable for acceleration — Best used as a first-pass tool with professional oversight
